# What is Velarix?

Velarix is the decision-integrity service for AI-assisted approvals and operational actions.

It sits at the execution boundary of your AI system and enforces four guarantees before any decision reaches production:

- **Facts are explicit** — every belief grounding a decision is recorded and queryable
- **Support is traceable** — you can follow the dependency chain from any conclusion back to its roots
- **Stale conclusions are retractable** — when a fact expires or is retracted, every downstream derived fact is automatically invalidated
- **Unsafe decisions are blocked** — governance policies check decisions at execution time, not after

## The Core Problem

AI agents make decisions based on context. That context goes stale. Without a truth-maintenance layer, agents execute on beliefs that were true at inference time but aren't true anymore — and nobody knows until something breaks.

Velarix solves this by maintaining a live dependency graph over facts. Every derived fact knows what it depends on. When a root fact changes, the graph propagates the invalidation forward immediately.

## What Ships

- **Go API** — facts, explanations, slices, governance, and first-class decisions
- **Symbolic reasoning engine** — dependency tracking and negated support
- **Python SDK** — runtime helpers for LangGraph, CrewAI, LlamaIndex, and OpenAI
- **`vlx` CLI** — core operational workflows
- **Benchmark harness** — reproducible contradiction and hallucination tests
